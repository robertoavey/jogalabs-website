## About rating stars (important honesty)

Star snippets require **real reviews**. Two hard rules from Google:
1. Since 2019 Google **ignores self-serving star markup** on LocalBusiness/Organization pages (a business marking up its own aggregate rating doesn't get stars in search).
2. Fabricated ratings with no visible reviews violate their guidelines and can trigger a manual penalty that hurts everything else we built.

The stars you actually CAN get:
- **Google Business Profile stars** — appear next to your name in Maps/local pack the moment you have reviews. This is the star display people recognize. → Phase 2 review engine.
- **Third-party review widgets** (e.g., embed Google reviews via a plugin like "Widgets for Google Reviews") — shows real stars ON your site, builds trust, no penalty risk.
- Once you have real reviews, we can add visible testimonials + matching Review markup to program pages — legitimate and helpful.
